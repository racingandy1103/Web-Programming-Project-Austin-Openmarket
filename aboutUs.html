About Us


Our goal with Austin OpenMarket is to promote environmentally-friendly second-hand shopping. Studies have shown that reusing and recycling products helps to reduce greenhouse emissions that contribute to global climate change. We create an economic incentive with low pricing to encourage people in the Austin community to buy or sell used items. Buying or selling second-hand clothes encourages sustainable fashion. Fashion is a water-intensive industry. For example, a crop like cotton requires a lot of water. Each cotton shirt requires approximately 2700 litres of water! By using Austin OpenMarket, not only can you help reduce water to save the environment, but you can find things that you actually need for a discounted price!
